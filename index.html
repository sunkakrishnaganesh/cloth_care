About Us

Our Mission

ClothCare is a non-profit organization dedicated to collecting and distributing gently used clothing to individuals and families in need. We believe that everyone deserves access to clean, warm clothing regardless of their financial situation.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small community initiative to a city-wide movement with hundreds of volunteers and thousands of donors.

How We Help

We partner with local shelters, schools, and community centers to identify those most in need. Our volunteers sort, clean, and distribute donated clothing to ensure dignity and quality for recipients.
